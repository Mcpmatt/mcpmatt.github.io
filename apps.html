Welcome to Matt's Final Project

This program is the Final Project for my Computer Science 106 class! It is a Space Invaders-style game, where you shoot missiles from a tank to destroy invading Alien Spaceships. The Left and Right Arrow Keys control the tank. You fire the missiles by holding down the SPACEBAR key. Click the Start Button to begin. Don't let the Aliens land!
